Valentine Pizza

Description

Make this pizza for your Valentine! The rosemary dough is made in a bread machine, and then made into three heart shaped pizzas.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1(.25ounce) envelope active dry yeast
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive, divided
  • 1 (14) can pizza sauce
  • 3 cups shredded mozzerela cheese
  • 2 ripe tomates, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 15 slices vegetarian pepperoni
  • 1 (2.25 ounce) can sliced black onlives
